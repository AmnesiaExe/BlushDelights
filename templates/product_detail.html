Raspberry Merlot

A smooth raspberry merlot mousse layered over a rich chocolate base, coated in glossy dark ganache and finished with fresh raspberries. Balanced with deep berry flavours and delicate sweetness, this elegant dessert delivers a luxurious and refined finish in every bite.

Product Info

Rich chocolate, sweet raspberry, and subtle berry wine notes.

Serves 1–2.

Allergens & Ingredients

Contains dairy, gluten, and eggs.

Made with raspberry, dark chocolate, cream, cocoa, flour, butter, sugar, eggs, and merlot reduction.

Storage

Keep refrigerated. Best enjoyed within 2 days.

Source

Crafted with locally sourced dairy and fresh farm-picked raspberries.
